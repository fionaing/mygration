About

Mygration is a website where birders can plan birding trips with others. Once a user is registered, they can find information on recent bird sightings around a trail and weather forecasts for the area. Users can add a bio to their public profile. Users can create a birding plan with information on the trail that they will follow, when they will go, and a description with additional information about the trip. A user can also post their planned trip publicly. By doing this, other users can join this user on their trip and give their opinion on the plan.

Make planning bird walks and meeting other birders easier with Mygration!
